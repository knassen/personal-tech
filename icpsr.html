ICPSR-related Sites/Social Research

"ICPSR" stands for the Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research. This non-profit, member-supported organization, located at the University of Michigan, maintains the world's largest social science data archive. The archive is accessible to all ICPSR member institutions via the World Wide Web and FTP (via their institution's ICPSR Official Representative). Non-members may view data collection abstracts and many of the codebooks. Below are a few of the web sites I've found useful in my work at ICPSR.
